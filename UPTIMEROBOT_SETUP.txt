UPTIMEROBOT SETUP - KEEP RENDER BACKEND AWAKE 24/7
===================================================

PROBLEM:
Render free tier spins down after 15 minutes of inactivity.
First request takes 30-60 seconds to wake up.

SOLUTION:
Use UptimeRobot to ping your backend every 5 minutes.
This keeps it awake 24/7 for FREE.

SETUP STEPS (2 MINUTES):
========================

1. Go to: https://uptimerobot.com

2. Click "Sign Up" (top right)
   - Use your email or Google account
   - FREE account (no credit card needed)

3. After signing in, click "Add New Monitor"

4. Fill in the form:
   
   Monitor Type: HTTP(s)
   ↓
   Friendly Name: SyncRoute Backend
   ↓
   URL (or IP): https://syncroute.onrender.com/api/health
   ↓
   Monitoring Interval: 5 minutes
   ↓
   Monitor Timeout: 30 seconds
   ↓
   Alert Contacts: (optional - your email)

5. Click "Create Monitor"

6. Done! ✅

WHAT HAPPENS NOW:
=================

✅ UptimeRobot pings your backend every 5 minutes
✅ Render stays awake (never spins down)
✅ Users never wait for wake-up
✅ Backend responds instantly
✅ Completely FREE forever

VERIFY IT'S WORKING:
====================

1. Go to UptimeRobot dashboard
2. You should see "SyncRoute Backend" with green "Up" status
3. Check "Response Time" - should be fast (< 1 second)
4. Check "Uptime" - should be 100%

OPTIONAL: ADD EMAIL ALERTS
===========================

If you want to be notified when backend goes down:

1. In UptimeRobot, click "My Settings"
2. Click "Add Alert Contact"
3. Enter your email
4. Verify email
5. Edit your monitor
6. Select your email in "Alert Contacts"
7. Save

Now you'll get email if backend goes down!
