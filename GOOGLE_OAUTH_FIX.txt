GOOGLE OAUTH REDIRECT_URI_MISMATCH FIX
========================================

PROBLEM:
Error 400: redirect_uri_mismatch when trying to sign in with Google

SOLUTION:
You need to configure the authorized redirect URIs in Google Cloud Console.

STEPS:

1. Go to Google Cloud Console:
   https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/credentials

2. Find your OAuth 2.0 Client ID:
   759769223324-3jqt5roenc8gf82qrsopddunlniugrcn

3. Click on it to edit

4. Add these to "Authorized JavaScript origins":
   - http://localhost:5173
   - https://syncroute.vercel.app
   - https://syncroute-ifa4e0h8j-majorprojectmvsr2025-8618s-projects.vercel.app

5. Add these to "Authorized redirect URIs":
   - http://localhost:5173
   - https://syncroute.vercel.app
   - https://syncroute-ifa4e0h8j-majorprojectmvsr2025-8618s-projects.vercel.app

6. Click "Save"

7. Wait 5 minutes for changes to propagate

8. Test Google Sign-In again

NOTE: The redirect URI must EXACTLY match your frontend URL (no trailing slashes, no /profile, no #documents)
