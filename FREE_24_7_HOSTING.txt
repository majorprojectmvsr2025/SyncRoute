FREE 24/7 HOSTING OPTIONS FOR BACKEND & DATABASE
================================================

CURRENT SETUP (What You Have Now):
===================================

✅ Frontend: Vercel (FREE, 24/7, unlimited)
⚠️ Backend: Render Free Tier (FREE, but spins down after 15 min inactivity)
⚠️ Database: MongoDB Atlas Free Tier (FREE, 24/7, but limited to 512MB)

THE PROBLEM WITH RENDER FREE TIER:
===================================

Render's free tier:
- ✅ FREE forever
- ✅ Automatic deployments
- ❌ Spins down after 15 minutes of inactivity
- ❌ Takes 30-60 seconds to wake up on first request
- ❌ 750 hours/month limit (not truly 24/7)

This means:
- First user each day waits 30-60 seconds
- After 15 min of no activity, backend sleeps
- Not ideal for production, but OK for development/testing

SOLUTIONS FOR TRUE 24/7 FREE HOSTING:
======================================

OPTION 1: Keep Render Awake (Recommended - Easiest)
----------------------------------------------------
Use a free service to ping your backend every 14 minutes:

Services:
1. UptimeRobot (https://uptimerobot.com)
   - FREE: 50 monitors
   - Ping every 5 minutes
   - Setup: 2 minutes

2. Cron-job.org (https://cron-job.org)
   - FREE: Unlimited jobs
   - Ping every 1 minute
   - Setup: 2 minutes

3. BetterUptime (https://betteruptime.com)
   - FREE: 10 monitors
   - Ping every 3 minutes
   - Setup: 2 minutes

HOW TO SET UP UPTIMEROBOT:
1. Go to https://uptimerobot.com
2. Sign up (free)
3. Click "Add New Monitor"
4. Monitor Type: HTTP(s)
5. Friendly Name: SyncRoute Backend
6. URL: https://syncroute.onrender.com/api/health
7. Monitoring Interval: 5 minutes
8. Click "Create Monitor"
9. Done! Your backend will never sleep.

PROS:
✅ Completely free
✅ Easy setup (2 minutes)
✅ Backend stays awake 24/7
✅ No code changes needed
✅ Works with current setup

CONS:
❌ Still limited to 750 hours/month on Render
❌ Not a "real" solution (just a workaround)

OPTION 2: Railway (Better than Render)
---------------------------------------
Railway offers better free tier than Render:

FREE TIER:
- $5 credit per month (enough for small apps)
- No spin-down (stays awake 24/7)
- Faster than Render
- Better dashboard

SETUP:
1. Go to https://railway.app
2. Sign up with GitHub
3. Click "New Project"
4. Select "Deploy from GitHub repo"
5. Choose your SyncRoute repo
6. Select syncroute-backend folder
7. Add environment variables (same as Render)
8. Deploy

MIGRATION TIME: 15 minutes

PROS:
✅ No spin-down (true 24/7)
✅ Faster than Render
✅ Better free tier
✅ Easy migration

CONS:
⚠️ $5/month credit (usually enough, but limited)
⚠️ After credit runs out, service stops

OPTION 3: Fly.io (Most Generous Free Tier)
-------------------------------------------
Fly.io has the most generous free tier:

FREE TIER:
- 3 shared-cpu VMs (256MB RAM each)
- 3GB persistent storage
- 160GB outbound data transfer
- No credit card required
- TRUE 24/7 (no spin-down)

SETUP:
1. Install flyctl: https://fly.io/docs/hands-on/install-flyctl/
2. Sign up: flyctl auth signup
3. In your backend folder: flyctl launch
4. Follow prompts
5. Deploy: flyctl deploy

MIGRATION TIME: 20 minutes

PROS:
✅ TRUE 24/7 (no spin-down)
✅ Most generous free tier
✅ No credit card required
✅ Fast global CDN
✅ Better than Render

CONS:
⚠️ Slightly more complex setup
⚠️ Requires CLI tool
